Welcome to L'Olivar Vell

Nestled in the heart of Barcelona's Gothic Quarter, L'Olivar Vell offers an exquisite journey through Mediterranean flavors. Our chef combines traditional Catalan recipes with modern culinary techniques, creating unforgettable dining experiences since 1987.
